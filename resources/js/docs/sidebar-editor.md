# 🎛️ Sidebar Editor

The **Sidebar Editor** lets you shape Peermitly's left navigation exactly how you like it. Hide the things you never use, group the rest into your own categories, rename and reorder them, and drag entries wherever they make sense to you. Your layout is saved automatically and stays that way across restarts.

> **Pro feature.** The Sidebar Editor is part of Peermitly **Pro**. On a Free plan you can still see your sidebar, but customizing it requires Pro.

There are two ways to tailor the sidebar — a quick on/off list, and the full drag-and-drop editor.

## ✅ Quick toggle: show or hide entries

Open **Settings → Sidebar**. Under **Sidebar items** you get a simple checklist of every navigation entry that can be hidden. Tick an entry to show it in the sidebar, untick it to hide it.

![The Sidebar tab in Settings with the item checklist](/images/screenshots/sidebar_1.png)

This is the fastest way to declutter: turn off the services and databases you don't use, and they disappear from the sidebar immediately. A handful of core entries (like **Home**, **Sites**, **Subscription** and **Settings**) always stay visible so you can never lock yourself out of the essentials.

To go further — grouping, renaming, reordering — click **Sidebar Editor**.

## 🧩 The full editor

The **Sidebar Editor** opens a full-screen board where every category is a box and every entry is a draggable card. The hint at the top says it all: **drag items between categories — changes save automatically**.

![The full Sidebar Editor with categories and items](/images/screenshots/sidebar_2.png)

Here's everything you can do:

### Move entries

Grab the handle on the left of any entry and **drag it** into another category — or to a new position within the same category. The sidebar updates to match.

### Show or hide a single entry

Each entry has an **eye icon**. Click it to hide that entry from the sidebar while keeping it in its category, and click again to show it. This is the per-item version of the checklist in Settings.

### Create and name categories

- Use the **Category name** field at the top of each box to rename it — call a group whatever you like (e.g. "My databases", "Daily tools").
- Click **Add category** at the bottom to create a new, empty group, then drag entries into it.

### Reorder and delete categories

- Drag a whole category box by its **handle** to change the order categories appear in the sidebar.
- Use the **trash icon** on a category to delete it. Any entries inside move to **Unused elements** rather than disappearing.

### Unused elements

At the very bottom sits the **Unused elements** tray: entries that aren't shown in the sidebar right now. Nothing here is lost — just **drag an entry out of the tray into any category** to bring it back. This is where deleted-category entries and fully hidden items collect.

## 💾 Saving, resetting and finishing

- **Automatic saving.** Every change — a move, a rename, a toggle — is saved instantly. There is no "Save" button to remember.
- **Reset to default.** Not happy with your layout? **Reset to default** restores Peermitly's original sidebar in one click.
- **Done.** When you're finished, click **Done** to leave the editor and return to your freshly arranged sidebar.

## 💡 Notes

- **Core entries stay put.** Essentials like Home, Sites, Subscription and Settings are always available and can't be hidden away.
- **Nothing is ever lost.** Hiding an entry or deleting a category just moves entries to **Unused elements** — drag them back any time.
- **It's personal.** Your sidebar layout is yours; it doesn't change how anything else in Peermitly works.
